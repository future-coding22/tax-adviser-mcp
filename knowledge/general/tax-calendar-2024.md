## Special Circumstances

### New Entrepreneur
- First year: May need to estimate income
- BTW: Quarterly filing usually assigned
- Provisional: Often no first-year assessment

### Moving to/from Netherlands
- Pro-rated tax year
- Special filing rules
- Different deadlines may apply
- Consult Belastingdienst

### Death
- Final return filed by estate
- Different deadlines
- Special rules apply
